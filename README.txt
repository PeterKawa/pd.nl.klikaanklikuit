Converting your home into a smart home? With KlikAanKlikUit you make every home smart, without complicated installations or conversion or shifting of existing switches and light points.

If you add wireless wall switches or other transmitters to your lighting, the existing (wired) wall switches will remain functional. You do not have to replace or remove it.

Connect all your KlikAanKlikUit devices to Homey and manage all your lighting and devices on your smartphone.
